Capabilities

Built for Enterprise
Precision.

Six core capabilities that transform how enterprises interact with AI.

Structured Synthesis

Multiple agents analyze your query from different angles, then synthesize their findings into a single, validated response with evidence trails.

Document-Aware

Upload contracts, reports, or datasets. Agents reason across your documents with full context retention — no token limits, no hallucinations.

Enterprise Security

Namespace isolation, OWASP LLM Top 10 compliance, output sanitization, and full audit logging. Built for regulated industries.

Audit Trails

Every decision is traceable. See which agents contributed, what evidence they cited, and how the final synthesis was reached.

Live Audio Agents

Real-time voice synthesis for live briefings, meetings, and presentations. AI agents that speak, not just type.

Project-Aware Context

Agents maintain awareness of your project scope, history, and goals across sessions. Continuity without repetition.
